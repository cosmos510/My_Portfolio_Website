Typing Speed Test

This is a simple application that allows you to check your typing speed. It offers two levels of difficulty and instruction screen. The app will keep track of your words per minute (WPM), net words per minute (NET WPM), characters per minute (CPM), and accuracy.

Morse Code Translator

Morse code is a method used in telecommunication to encode text character as standardized sequences of two different signal durations, called dots and dashes. Features included: Convert text to morse code, convert morse code to text and Play morse code.
